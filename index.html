Game format

The fairest
format.

❌ Old system
Classic match
1st and 4th in the table played against 2nd and 3rd
If your partner had a bad day, you lost too
Constant complaints about partners
Result depended too much on who you were paired with
✅ New system
Everyone vs everyone
Everyone plays with and against everyone in the group
Points are individual, not by pair
Each set won = 1 point. Max 3, min 0
The ranking reflects your real level — no excuses
01
You sign up solo — no partner needed
Registration is individual. You don't need to come with a partner. The system organises the groups and you play with and against everyone in your group.
02
Blind draws — you find out 48h before
Your opponents are revealed only 2 days before the match. Until then, you don't know who you're playing. No favouritism, no special prep. Everyone on equal terms.
03
2 vs 2 format — 90 minutes on court
4 players, 90 minutes, everyone plays with and against everyone. Sets start at 2-2 (not 0-0) so there's time to play all rotations. First to reach 6 wins the set. If it reaches 6-6, a normal tie-break decides it.
04
Set won → 1 point. Then pairs switch.
The winning pair each earn 1 individual point. Then pairs rotate — your previous opponents become your partners. A new set starts again from 2-2.
Pair A wins · +1 point each Pair B loses · 0 points
05
As many sets as time allows — max 3 points
You keep rotating and playing until the 90 minutes are up. Each player ends the session with between 0 and 3 points depending on how many sets they won. Your ranking is yours alone — no partner excuses.
3 pts · Perfect session 2 pts · Good session 1 pt · Average 0 pts · Tough day
What about the court fee? Court costs are not included in the season fee. Each player pays their share of the court on match day, depending on the club. The registration fee covers your spot in the league, your individual ranking and access to the private community.
