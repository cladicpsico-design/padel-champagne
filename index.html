What is Padel Champagne?

More than a league. A community of 150+ players who share a passion for padel and good vibes.

🏆

The biggest amateur league

The largest amateur padel league in the Frankfurt / Rhein-Main region. 5 skill groups, 10 weeks per season, 4 seasons per year.

👤

Individual signup

No partner needed. You sign up alone, we assign your rivals based on standings. Just show up and play.

🎯

For everyone

From absolute beginners to top 50 Germany. We have a group for every level. You'll always play against people at your skill.
