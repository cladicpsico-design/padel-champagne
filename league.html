THE LEAGUE

Everything you need to know about how we play

How it works

Simple format, maximum fun. Here's everything you need to know.

🎭

American Rotation

4 players, 3 sets. Pairs rotate every set so you play with and against everyone.

Set 1 A+B vs C+D
Set 2 A+C vs B+D
Set 3 A+D vs B+C
🏆

Scoring

Sets start at 0-0. First to 6 games wins the set.

At deuce (40-40) there is no advantage — it's golden point. One decisive point decides the game.

Each set you win = 1 point. You can earn 0 to 3 points per match.

📅

Before the match

Rivals are revealed 48 hours before your match, based on current standings.

You'll know exactly who you're playing with and against.

👤

Individual signup

No partner needed. You sign up alone — we handle the rest.

Just show up, play, and enjoy.

📅

Schedule

Matches are played throughout the week, mainly in the afternoon and evening during prime time hours.

🔄

10 weeks of season

Up to 2 matches per week, meaning up to 20 matches per season. Each match takes about 90 minutes including warm-up.

📈

Promotions & relegations

The top 2 players of each group move up. The bottom players move down. Your performance determines your level.

A playoff may also take place depending on the groups and levels.

🌟

Flexibility

Players can make their own court bookings and use them for the league. The idea is to make it as flexible as possible for everyone.

🌧

Rain policy

Outdoor matches with rain forecast 48h before will be cancelled by default. If all players agree to play anyway, the match goes ahead — but they cover the court cost themselves.

⛔

Cancellations

Matches cannot be cancelled with less than 24 hours notice, except for force majeure. If cancelled last-minute without a valid reason, the player covers the court cost and a substitute will be found.
